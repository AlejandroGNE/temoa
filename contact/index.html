Temoa Mailing List

We would prefer that any question about using Temoa be asked on the mailing list. We will of course answer any personal email we receive, but the list enables us to keep a publicly available archive of discussions around Temoa, as well as having more people look at a specific problem you may encounter. This list is open, but it requires a subscription before you may send an email to it.

Please note that we are academics, not spammers. You will receive email from this list only as long as you are subscribed. Subscription is entirely self-managed, so you may subscribe yourself to – and unsubscribe yourself from – the list via the above link.
